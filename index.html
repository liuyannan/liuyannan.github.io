Biography

Yannan Liu is a Security Expert at Sangfor Technologies Inc. He received his Ph.D. degree in the department of Computer Science and Engineering of The Chinese University of Hong Kong. Before that, he received the B.Eng degree in 2012 from college of Computer Science and Technology of Zhejiang Univiersity.

His research interest includes hardware security, malware detection and analysis, security of deep neural network, and computer architecture.
