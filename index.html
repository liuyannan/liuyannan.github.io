Biography

I am a Ph.D student (year 4 and post-candidate) in the department of Computer Science and Engineering of The Chinese University of Hong Kong. My supervisor is Prof. Qiang Xu. Before that, I received the B.Eng degree in 2012 from Colleage of Computer Science and Technology of Zhejiang Univiersity.

My research interest includes hardware security, computer system security, deep neural network, internet of things, and computer architecture.
