Biography

I am a member of Wuheng Lab in Bytedance Inc. (CV is here) . I used to work as a Security Technical Expert at Sangfor Technologies Inc, where I investigated AI-based malware detection. I received my Ph.D. degree in the department of Computer Science and Engineering of The Chinese University of Hong Kong. Before that, I received the B.Eng degree in 2012 from college of Computer Science and Technology of Zhejiang Univiersity.

My current research focuses on both AI for security and security for AI. Especially, I am very interested in speeding up information flow in cyber security.
